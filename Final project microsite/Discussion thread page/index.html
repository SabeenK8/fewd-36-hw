Hi Mariam! Thanks for creating this thread. I have been in the mHealth market for several years now, and I can tell you that the FDA’s guidelines do not affect the performance of my technology. It is, however, important to take the brand messaging away from personal health management and more into tracking one’s health, though not for purposes of self diagnosis of automating treatment.

I am also interested to know who else has a reaction to the guidelines, and how their app is impacted?
